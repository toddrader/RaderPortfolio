Todd Rader

Strategic communications. Gets it. Gets it done.

I was in a movie with Al Pacino. He was 'Dr. Death' Jack Kevorkian. I was a photographer. I played a Chicago cop on TV. In the mob movie, 'Kill the Irishman', Vincent D'Onofrio and I, and 200 other guys, were dockworkers.

At work and at play, I take on many roles to support team. I am a strategic and creative marketer who gets it and gets it done.

As an Engagement Strategist and Project Manager, I see projects through the eyes and experiences of customers. I connect all the dots in their journey – planning a course from where they are to where we’d like them to go – from quality of life insight to the desired result. Part UX architect, part media surveyor, part content manager, part go-to-market planner, part PM, I manage to make it all work, while usually wearing a vintage bowling shirt with another guy’s name on it.

I have enjoyed roles on all three sides of the ball: client, agency and production. My experiences provide a deeper understanding and achieve greater results.

I was recognized as Employee of the Year at Campbell Ewald for innovations in workflow and technology while managing a team of 22 direct reports.
